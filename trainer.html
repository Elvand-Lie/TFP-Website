JOSE HE

(何德伟)

Growing up in a family of geomancy practitioners, Jose was exposed to ancient metaphysical wisdom from a young age. Despite this early exposure, he was initially reluctant to practise the discipline, believing it to be largely psychological in nature rather than a practical tool for real-world decision-making.

From Crisis to Clarity.

The Turning Point (At 35)

Jose experienced the most challenging period of his life—facing over half a million dollars in debt, the breakdown of a long-term relationship, and the loss of his home. During this time, his family guided him through the structured application of Chinese metaphysical principles to reassess his direction.

Within two years, he successfully cleared his debts and rebuilt stability in his life. This firsthand experience shifted his perspective entirely—from scepticism to conviction. Recognising the tangible impact of these time-tested systems when applied correctly, Jose spent the following decade studying, refining, and practising Chinese Metaphysics.

Today, Jose founded The Full Picture with a clear mission: to provide clarity, alignment, and actionable strategies that empower individuals and businesses to make informed decisions and create meaningful, sustainable transformation in their lives.
